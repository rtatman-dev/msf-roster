Privacy Policy

MSF Roster Analyzer  ·  Operated by SuperZero  ·  Last updated: April 2026

Overview

MSF Roster Analyzer is a personal, non-commercial tool built by SuperZero for the purpose of reviewing and analyzing a Marvel Strike Force character roster using AI. This policy explains what data is used, how it is handled, and your rights as a user.

Who operates this app

This app is operated by a single individual using the gamertag SuperZero. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Scopely, Boundless Entertainment, or Marvel Entertainment.

What data this app accesses

When you use this app, the following data may be accessed:

  • Your Marvel Strike Force player profile (name, level, power rating)
  • Your character roster (character names, gear tiers, star levels, power scores)
  • Your MSF API key, which you enter manually and is used only to fetch the above data

How your data is used

Your roster data is used solely to display your characters in the app and to generate AI-powered analysis and recommendations. Data is sent to the Claude AI API (operated by Anthropic) for this analysis.

Your data is not sold, shared with third parties for commercial purposes, stored on any server, or retained after your browser session ends. No account is created and no personal data is collected by this app.

Your API key

Your MSF API key is entered by you and used only within your browser to make requests to the Marvel Strike Force API. It is never transmitted to or stored by this app or its operator. You can revoke your API key at any time via the MSF developer dashboard.

Third-party services

This app communicates with the following external services:

  • Marvel Strike Force API (hub.marvelstrikeforce.com) — to retrieve your roster data. Subject to Scopely's own privacy policy and terms of service.
  • Anthropic Claude API (api.anthropic.com) — to generate AI analysis of your roster. Your roster summary is sent as part of this request. Subject to Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

Cookies and tracking

This app does not use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels, or any form of user tracking.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children. Access requires a Marvel Strike Force account, which is subject to Scopely's own age requirements.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the change. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions?
This app is a personal project. For questions or concerns about this privacy policy, you can reach SuperZero via the Marvel Strike Force community Discord at discord.gg/marvelstrikeforce.
